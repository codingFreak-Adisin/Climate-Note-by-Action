Climate Note

by Action For World

We Are Action For World A Youth-Led Non Profit Organization Working Towards Spreading Awareness About Climate Change, Educating About Climate Change And Environmental Science. We Work On Creating Educational Resources, Course Websites And Running A Community For People Who Are Passionate About Climate. We Are Not Just A Organization We Are A Group Of Teens Coming Together As One To Solve The Climate Crisis. Outside Of Climate Change Courses, We Use Various Mediums To Provide Education To All. For Instance, We Run Action Chat Community, Action Climate Blogs, Action Conferencing App And An Active Instagram.
