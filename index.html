Climate
Course

This is a course, that will teach you all you should basically know about climate change and what major steps to take to improve climate change situations right now. The videos are of 3 – 4 minutes long and completion of course will take you no more than 30 – 40 mins. After every single video, there will be a quiz based on the content of the video, to test your knowledge. You can even access our chat community full of people, climate and sustainability experts who are interested in the same cause as you, who you can talk, network etc. Soon we will have small events every month in the community too which can help you find connections, learn more, share your opinions etc. Our development side is currently working on the chat community
